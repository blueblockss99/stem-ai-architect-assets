Sample Capstones

Smart Sorter Bot

Classifies materials using image recognition. (AI Focus: Computer Vision)

Energy Optimizer Dashboard

Detects anomalies and suggests efficiency improvements using smart home data. (AI Focus: Predictive Analytics)

Bridge Safety Monitor

Simulates a structural health system that detects vibration patterns or surface wear. (AI Focus: Sensor data analysis)

Traffic Flow Simulator

Predicts congestion and proposes timing adjustments for smart intersections. (AI Focus: Predictive Modeling)

Starter Mini-Labs

Object Classifier for Automation

Tool: Teachable Machine. Focus: Robotics sorting logic.

Prototype Designer

Tool: Tinkercad + Canva. Focus: Visual mockup of AI-assisted systems.

System Predictor

Tool: Lobe / Excel. Focus: Predicting system performance from sensor data.

Core Tool Stack

No-Code AI: Teachable Machine, Lobe, ChatGPT, (Runway ML, AutoML)

Simulation & Design: Tinkercad, Scratch for IoT logic, (Fusion 360, Simulink)

Visualization: Canva, Datawrapper, Power BI (Copilot), (Tableau Public)

The stack ensures prototyping and demonstration without heavy infrastructure.

Data & Safety Rules

  • Only simulated or anonymized data.
  • Use digital twins (virtual representations) over live robotics.
  • Emphasize ethical AI in automation (fairness, safety, transparency).

SME Review & Mentorship

Cohorts are paired with an Engineering Subject Matter Expert (SME) who reviews scenario design accuracy and safety compliance. Mentors host "Design Review Fridays" and focus on translating AI predictions into real-world design improvements.

Certification & Portfolio

Participants earn the AI + Engineering Specialist Certificate and complete an AI-powered engineering capstone. Graduates add three verified mini-labs and a mentor-reviewed capstone to their public portfolio.
