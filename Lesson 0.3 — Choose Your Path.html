Lesson 0.3 — Choose Your Path

Purpose: Find Your AI Story

This lesson helps you choose the specialization lens through which you’ll complete your certification capstone. You will select one primary domain — **Medicine, Engineering, Business & Finance, or the Arts** — that connects your background to AI in a meaningful way.

You’re not just choosing a topic — you’re choosing your story for how AI education comes alive in your world.

Lesson Overview

  • • Explore four specialization pathways and sample capstone ideas.
  • • Identify which path aligns best with your background, curiosity, or teaching context.
  • • Complete a short reflection which you will use to participate in the **Discussion Forum**.
  • • Review your specialization’s One-Pager (which includes project examples and resources).
  • • Mark this lesson complete to unlock Module 1: Foundations & Pedagogy.

The Four Specialization Pathways

Review each pathway to choose your specialization lens.

1. AI + Medicine Lens

Theme: Using AI to make health and wellness learning accessible and ethical.

Example Capstones:

  • AI Triage Chatbot for School Nurses
  • Predicting Asthma Triggers with Wearables

You’ll Learn To:

  • Integrate data ethics and privacy in health-related lessons.
  • Use AI for pattern recognition and wellness simulations.
  • Teach health students to ask ethical AI questions.

2. AI + Engineering Lens

Theme: Solving real-world design challenges using AI and sensors.

Example Capstones:

  • Predictive Maintenance for School Buses
  • Smart Bridge Stress Detector

You’ll Learn To:

  • Connect AI with IoT and environmental monitoring.
  • Use visual AI for structural or mechanical data.
  • Encourage analytical thinking through simulation.

3. AI + Arts & Humanities Lens

Theme: Exploring creativity, emotion, and culture through AI.

Example Capstones:

  • AI-Generated Soundscapes for Theater
  • Style Transfer in Art History Lessons

You’ll Learn To:

  • Use generative AI to explore storytelling, music, and visual expression.
  • Discuss ethics and authorship in creative AI.
  • Foster creativity through cross-disciplinary studio projects.

4. AI + Business & Finance Lens

Theme: Teaching strategy, data literacy, and decision-making with AI.

Example Capstones:

  • Demand Forecasting for Cafeteria Menus
  • Ethical AI Pricing Simulator

You’ll Learn To:

  • Apply AI for forecasting and optimization in everyday contexts.
  • Discuss ethical data use and automation in business.
  • Guide students in interpreting AI-driven insights.

How to Choose Your AI Adventure

Imagine your passion as a glowing star and your students’ curiosity as a constellation. Where they overlap, that’s your path.

Ask yourself these 3 magic questions:

1. What lights me up at 3 PM on a Friday? (The subject you could talk about for hours.)
2. What makes my students lean in and whisper “Whoa”? (The topic they beg to explore.)
3. What if AI could make this moment 10× more alive? (Picture your dream lesson — now add AI magic.)

Still torn? Spin the AI Compass:

You Love… → Try This Path
Healing & caring AI + Medicine (Heartbeats + algorithms)
Building & tinkering AI + Engineering (Sensors + superpowers)
Creating & expressing AI + Arts (Pixels + poetry)
Money & strategy AI + Business (Data + decisions)

Pro tip: Pick the one closest to your daily reality. You’re not locked in; paths weave together. You can switch anytime before Week 3.
