Capstone & Mini-Lab Projects

Sample Capstones

Smart Sales Forecaster

Students design an AI model to predict product demand and visualize results in Power BI. (AI Focus: Predictive analytics)

Brand Sentiment Analyzer

A chatbot or dashboard that classifies customer feedback as positive, neutral, or negative. (AI Focus: Natural language processing)

Startup Success Simulator

Students simulate business growth scenarios using AI-generated insights. (AI Focus: Scenario modeling)

Sustainable Finance Explorer

Analyzes sustainability reports to identify ethical investment trends. (AI Focus: Data mining & ethical AI)

Each capstone emphasizes both quantitative skills and ethical reflection, preparing educators to guide real-world student projects.

Starter Mini-Labs

Trend Tracker

Tool: Datawrapper + Copilot. Focus: Visualize sample sales or market data and discuss what AI can “see.”

Customer Voice Classifier

Tool: Teachable Machine. Focus: Train a small model to detect satisfaction from text samples.

AI Pitch Coach

Tool: ChatGPT + Canva. Focus: Generate startup ideas and AI-driven business model posters.

Impact Scoring

Tool: Power BI (Copilot). Focus: Simulate how sustainability choices affect profitability metrics.

Each lab builds understanding of AI as a decision-support tool, not a replacement for human creativity.

Data & Ethics

  • Use synthetic or public business datasets (e.g., sample retail, financial, or climate data).
  • Discuss privacy, bias, and fairness in automated decision systems.
  • Highlight responsible AI in marketing and finance — avoiding manipulation or misinformation.
  • Reinforce human oversight in financial predictions and automated decisions.
  • Ethical reflection journals are encouraged throughout the specialization.

SME Checkpoints & Mentorship

  • Model Validation:

    • Ensures logical accuracy in AI-driven analysis and market realism.

  • Ethical Oversight:

    • Reviews AI applications for fairness, inclusion, and transparency.

Mentors emphasize critical thinking, financial storytelling, and data empathy — teaching how to balance precision with people.
