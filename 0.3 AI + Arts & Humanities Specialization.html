Capstone & Mini-Lab Projects

Sample Capstones

“The Human Touch” Gallery

Students create an AI-generated art exhibit exploring emotion, culture, and style. (AI Focus: Image generation & style transfer)

StoryWeaver

An interactive story written collaboratively by students and ChatGPT — exploring ethical dilemmas or cultural narratives. (AI Focus: Generative text & storytelling)

AI Persona Dialogues

Students train character prompts and record simulated interviews with historical or fictional figures. (AI Focus: Conversational AI & role simulation)

Synesthetic Soundscapes

Students generate music inspired by visual themes or data-driven emotion analysis. (AI Focus: Audio synthesis & multimodal creativity)

Starter Mini-Labs

Style Remix Studio

Tool: Canva + Runway ML. Focus: Experiment with reimagining art styles using AI filters.

Creative Prompt Jam

Tool: ChatGPT + Canva. Focus: Generate story ideas or poems using creative prompt engineering.

Bias in Portraits

Tool: DALL·E + Discussion Cards. Focus: Explore bias in generated human portraits and discuss representation.

Data-to-Music

Tool: Google MusicLM / Runway ML. Focus: Turn emotion words or text prompts into soundscapes.

Every capstone and mini-lab integrates creative production with critical reflection, ensuring that students question as much as they create.

Data & Ethics Rules

  • Use only copyright-free or AI-generated content for classroom projects.
  • Teach authorship and attribution in AI-assisted works.
  • Encourage reflection on bias, originality, and human intent in creative outputs.
  • Discuss AI’s cultural impact — from deepfakes to digital storytelling.
  • Emphasize AI as a co-creator, not a replacement.
